## Initial Setup
1. After installing <a href="https://github.com/TheNathannator/WiitarThing/releases" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">[WiitarThing]</a> along with <a href="https://github.com/nefarius/ViGEmBus/releases" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">[ViGEmBus]</a>, open it up.

2. Press the **`[SYNC]`** button at the top left.  
<Image src={wiitarsyncbut} alt="WiitarThing with a cursor hovering over the red 'SYNC' button." />

3. Press either the red `SYNC` button on the back of your Wii Remote<sup>a</sup> or press both `1+2` at the same time<sup>b</sup>. You may have to try this a few times.  
* A:<Image src={wiimotesync12} alt="A Wii Remote with the 1 and 2 buttons being highlighted." />  
* B:<Image src={wiimotesyncbut} alt="A Wii Remote with the 'SYNC' button the back being highlighted." />

4. Once it's synced, click **`[CONNECT]`**.  
<Image src={wiitarconnectbut} alt="WiitarThing with a cursor hovering over the 'CONNECT' button." />

5. Map your guitar.

<details>
<summary>How do I map my guitar?</summary>
**Click on the `Pads` icon at the top of RPCS3**.  
<Image src={rpcs3pad} alt="The Pads menu in RPCS3." />

It’s suggested to create a new configuration by clicking `Add Configuration` at the top right of the `Pads` window.  
This is so you can change configuration profiles in case you want to play other games.  
<Image src={rpcs3padprofadd} alt="The top right of RPCS3's Pads window. 'Add Configuration' is being clicked on by the mouse cursor" />
Follow the instructions and/or match the assignments below.

## Mapping
By default, XInput has most things bound correctly. You only need to adjust the following:

**UNMAP THE FOLLOWING** or guitar solos will auto-strum!  
* Use Right Click to unmap a button.

| **RPCS3** | **XInput** | **Guitar** |
|:--------:|:-----------:|:-----------:|
| L2 | ![Left Trigger](/assets/instruments/buttons/360/lt.png "Left Trigger") | Solo Modifier |

**Change the following** or blue and yellow frets will be inverted!

| **RPCS3** | **XInput** | **Guitar** |
|:--------:|:-----------:|:-----------:|
| Square | ![Y Button](/assets/instruments/buttons/360/y.png "Y Button") | ![Yellow Fret](/assets/instruments/buttons/guitars/yf.png "Yellow Fret") | 
| Triangle | ![X Button](/assets/instruments/buttons/360/x.png "X Button") | ![Blue Fret](/assets/instruments/buttons/guitars/bf.png "Blue Fret") |

Guitar Hero controllers tend to misbehave and **refuse to map sometimes. If you try mapping a button and always get `U+` or something similar, click `Filter Noise`** at the bottom left of the controller configuration window **then try mapping**.

## Profile

<details>
<summary>How do I use profiles?</summary>

<Aside type="caution">This is not recommended if you're using multiple controllers at once.</Aside>

After downloading the profile,

1. Extract the .7z file.
2. Drag the `input_configs` folder into the `configs` folder where you have RPCS3 in.  
><Image src={instrepoinstall} alt="Installing a configuration profile from the Instrument Repo" />

3. After that, you can select the profile in the `Pads` menu.  
><Image src={rpcs3padprofile} alt="RPCS3's Gamepad Settings, showing the cursor over a profile." />

Most of the time, these controller profiles should work out of the box. If they don’t, try changing the controller listed in `Devices`, next to the `Refresh` button until it receives an input. You can edit this while the game is running.
</details>
