### Initial Setup
* For the best experience:
<Steps>
1. Go to the Start menu and look for `Set up USB game controllers`, then open it.  
	> <Image src={joycpl} alt="Windows showing the search results of joy.cpl." />

2. Select `Guitar Hero X-plorer` then click on `Properties`.  
	> <Image src={joy1} alt="Windows's Game Controllers Setup showing a list of installed game controllers." />

3. Go to the `Settings` tab.  
	> <Image src={joy2} alt="The X-plorer's properties with a cursor over the 'Settings' tab." />

4. Hold the guitar at a 45 degree angle. This is halfway between a neck at level and full tilt.  
	> <Image src={gtr45} alt="An X-plorer being held at a 45 degree angle." />

5. While holding the guitar at this angle, click `Calibrate`.  
	> <Image src={joy3} alt="The X-plorer's properties with the cursor over 'Calibrate'." />

6. Click `Next` on the D-Pad screen with no buttons held.  
	> <Image src={joy5} alt="Game Device Calibration on the 'Find Center Point' with the cursor on 'Next'." />

7. On the next screen, press the whammy all the way in then release it, then click `Next`.  
	> <Image src={joy6} alt="Game Device Calibration on the 'Axis Calibration' for the slider axis with the cursor on 'Next'." />

8. On the next screen, move the guitar to full tilt position and then click `Next`.  
	> <Image src={gtr90} alt="An X-plorer being held at a 90 degree angle." />  

	> <Image src={joy7} alt="Game Device Calibration on the 'Axis Calibration' for the Z axis with the cursor on 'Next'." />

9. After that, click on `Finish`.  
	> <Image src={joy8} alt="The end of the Game Device Calibration." />  

</Steps>

## Profile

<details>
<summary>How do I use profiles?</summary>

<Aside type="caution">This is not recommended if you're using multiple controllers at once.</Aside>

After downloading the profile,

1. Extract the .7z file.
2. Drag the `input_configs` folder into the `configs` folder where you have RPCS3 in.  
> <Image src={instrepoinstall} alt="Installing a configuration profile from the Instrument Repo" />

3. After that, you can select the profile in the **“Pads”** menu.  
> <Image src={rpcs3padprofile} alt="RPCS3's Gamepad Settings, showing the cursor over a profile." />

Most of the time, these controller profiles should work out of the box. If they don’t, try changing the controller listed in `Devices`, next to the `Refresh` button until it receives an input. You can edit this while the game is running.
</details>
