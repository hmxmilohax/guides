Do NOT map this instrument via the `Pads` menu!
</Aside>
<Aside type="caution">RPCN Menus (for sending or accepting online invites) **will** cause a softlock. You will need an alternative input method to navigate these menus, such as a <a href="../../main#gamepads" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">[typing keyboard or a gamepad.]</a></Aside>

* Rock Band 1 guitars **require their respective guitar dongles to connect to your computer.**  
Rock Band 1 guitars have a maple (cream color) neck with a black dongle.  
![Black PlayStation 3 Rock Band Guitar Dongle](/assets/instruments/adapters/ps3guitarrb1.png "Black PlayStation 3 Rock Band Drums Dongle")
Model#: `8221512`  
* Rock Band 2 (and later) guitars **require their respective guitar dongles to connect to your computer.**  
Rock Band 2 and The Beatles: Rock Band drum dongles are compatible with either revisions.    
![Black PlayStation 3 Rock Band Guitar Dongle](/assets/instruments/adapters/ps3guitarrb2.png "Black PlayStation 3 Rock Band Guitar Dongle")
Model#: `PGTSELEA2B`  
![Grey PlayStation 3 Rock Band Guitar Dongle](/assets/instruments/adapters/ps3guitarrb2gry.png "Grey PlayStation 3 Rock Band Guitar Dongle")
Model#: `PGTSELEA2B`  
![PlayStation 3 The Beatles: Rock Band Guitar Dongle](/assets/instruments/adapters/ps3guitarbeatles.png "PlayStation 3 The Beatles: Rock Band Guitar Dongle")
Model#: `PGTSELEA3B`

There is no configuration for this instrument. Simply connect the receiver to your computer and that's it!
