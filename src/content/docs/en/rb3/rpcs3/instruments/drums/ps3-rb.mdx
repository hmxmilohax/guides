Do NOT map this instrument via the `Pads` menu!
</Aside>
<Aside type="caution">RPCN Menus (for sending or accepting online invites) **will** cause a softlock. You will need an alternative input method to navigate these menus, such as a <a href="../../main#gamepads" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">[typing keyboard or a gamepad.]</a></Aside>

* Rock Band 1 Drums can be connected via USB. Rock Band 1 drums are black with grey pads and lack the connectors for cymbals. 
* Rock Band 2 (and later) Drums **require their respective drum dongles to connect to your computer.**  
Rock Band 2 and The Beatles: Rock Band drum dongles are compatible with either revisions.  
![Black PlayStation 3 Rock Band Drums Dongle](/assets/instruments/adapters/ps3drumsrb2blk.png "Black PlayStation 3 Rock Band Drums Dongle")
Model#: `PDMSELEA2B`  
![Grey PlayStation 3 Rock Band Drums Dongle](/assets/instruments/adapters/ps3drumsrb2gry.png "Grey PlayStation 3 Rock Band Drums Dongle")
Model#: `PDMSELEA2B`  
![PlayStation 3 The Beatles: Rock Band Drums Dongle](/assets/instruments/adapters/ps3drumsbeatles.png "PlayStation 3 The Beatles: Rock Band Drums Dongle")
Model#: `PDMSELEA3B`

There is no configuration for this instrument. Simply connect the receiver to your computer and that's it!
