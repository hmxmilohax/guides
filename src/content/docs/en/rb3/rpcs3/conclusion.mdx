You now (hopefully) have set up Rock Band 3 on your PC. While you're here, why not join some communities that are helping with keeping the Rock Band community alive?

## MiloHax
<a href="https://milohax.org/" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer"><Image src={milohax} alt="MiloHax Logo" /></a>  
MiloHax is a community centered around modding and preserving the legacy of Harmonix's video games. It has been involved in everything from <a href="https://hiddenpalace.org/Rock_Band_3_(Jan_19,_2010)" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">[hunting down and preserving prototype copies]</a>, <a href="https://decomp.dev/DarkRTA/rb3" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">[decompilation]</a>, to being involved in helping emulators such as RPCS3 or shadPS4 improve how they run Harmonix games.  
<a href="https://discord.gg/milohax" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">You can <strong>[join the MiloHax Discord here]</strong></a>.

## RBEnhanced
<a href="https://rb3e.rbenhanced.rocks/" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer"><Image src={rbenhanced} alt="RBEnhanced Logo" /></a>  
Developers of the amazing RBEnhanced mod. The same developers also helped make, run, and maintain the GoCentral server that is the online revival server for Rock Band 3. Without this, this game would've been a lot lonelier and this guide wouldn't have existed.  
You can <a href="https://discord.gg/6rRUWXPYwb" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer"><strong>[join RBEnhanced’s Discord here]</strong></a>.

## RPCS3
<a href="https://rpcs3.net/about/" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer"><Image src={rpcs3} alt="RPCS3 Logo" /></a>  
It goes without saying but you wouldn't be playing Rock Band 3 on your PC at the moment without the tremendous work of the talented developers of RPCS3. The amount of work and attention the developers have put into just helping us get this funny plastic instrument game working is tremendous and we're all thankful for it.  
You can <a href="https://discord.gg/RPCS3" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer"><strong>[join RPCS3's Discord here]</strong></a>.
